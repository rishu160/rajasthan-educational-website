About the College

Government College Jaipur is one of the leading educational institutions in rural Rajasthan, established in 1985. The college is committed to providing quality higher education to students from rural and semi-urban areas. With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty, we offer comprehensive programs in Arts, Science, and Commerce streams.
